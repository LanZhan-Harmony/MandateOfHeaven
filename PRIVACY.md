# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 8, 2026

## English

### Overview

**Mandate Of Heaven** ("the Game", "we", "us", or "our") is an FMV (Full Motion Video) interactive game. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you play the Game.

### Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.** We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

### Game Save Data

To provide a seamless gaming experience, your game progress (save data) is stored on our servers. Save data includes:

- Your game progress (visited storylets, selected choices, timeline history)
- Gameplay states and statistics derived from your choices

This data is associated with an anonymous session identifier (a cookie stored in your browser) rather than a personal account. It is used solely to save and load your game progress.

### Session Cookie

The Game uses a session cookie (`_session`) to identify your save data. This cookie is strictly functional — it does not track your activity across other websites or services.

### Local Storage

The Game also stores your save ID locally on your device (via `localStorage`) so it can retrieve the correct save from the server on your next visit.

### Third-Party Services

The Game does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

### Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your gameplay data to any third parties, except as required by applicable law.

### Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). The anonymous save data described above does not constitute personal information.

### Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted here with a revised "Last updated" date.

### Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the development team through the official channels of the Game's publisher.

## 中文

### 概述

《江山北望》（以下简称"本游戏"）是一款 FMV（全动态影像）互动影游。本隐私政策说明您在游玩本游戏时我们如何处理您的信息。

### 我们收集的信息

**我们不收集任何个人信息。** 我们不会收集您的姓名、电子邮箱、电话号码或任何其他可识别个人身份的信息。

### 游戏存档数据

为了提供无缝的游戏体验，您的游戏进度（存档数据）会存储在我们的服务器上。存档数据包括：

- 游戏进度（已访问的剧情节点、已做出的选择、时间轴历史）
- 由您的选择产生的游戏状态及统计数据

这些数据通过一个匿名的会话标识符（存储在您浏览器中的 cookie）关联，而非个人账户。存档数据仅用于保存和加载您的游戏进度。

### 会话 Cookie

本游戏使用一个会话 Cookie（`_session`）来标识您的存档数据。该 Cookie 纯属功能性质 —— 不会跨其他网站或服务追踪您的活动。

### 本地存储

本游戏还会在您的设备本地（通过 `localStorage`）存储存档 ID，以便您下次访问时从服务器加载正确的存档。

### 第三方服务

本游戏未集成任何第三方分析、广告或跟踪服务。

### 数据共享

我们不会向任何第三方出售、共享或披露您的游戏数据，除非适用法律另有要求。

### 儿童隐私

本游戏不会故意收集任何未满 13 周岁（或您所在司法管辖区的适用同意年龄）儿童的个人信息。上述匿名存档数据不构成个人信息。

### 政策变更

如果本隐私政策将来发生变更，更新后的版本将发布在此处，并附上修订后的"最后更新"日期。

### 联系方式

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过游戏出品方的官方渠道联系开发团队。
